Amazon Echo

The Amazon Echo is a smart speaker that responds to voice commands using Alexa. It can play music, answer questions, set timers, and control connected smart home devices.

Amazon Kindle

The Amazon Kindle is a series of e-readers designed and marketed by Amazon. Kindle devices enable users to browse, buy, download, and read e-books, newspapers, magazines and other digital media.

Fire TV Stick (3rd Gen)

The 3rd Gen Fire TV Stick delivers powerful HD streaming through its quad-core 1.7 GHz processor and IMG GE8300 GPU. Control your TV and soundbar with the Alexa Voice Remote, which also offers voice search and quick-launch buttons for your favorite apps. Enjoy access to over a million movies, TV episodes, live content, and music from popular platforms like Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, and YouTube TV. Immerse yourself in Dolby Atmos audio on compatible systems, and benefit from Bluetooth 5.0 compatibility for pairing with speakers, headphones, and game controllers. The device offers 8 GB of internal storage, free cloud storage for Amazon digital content, and supports 802.11a/b/g/n/ac Wi-Fi networks for fast streaming and fewer dropped connections. Customize your viewing experience with accessibility features, including VoiceView, Screen Magnifier, and Text Banner. Note: 4K streaming is not supported; for this feature, consider the Fire TV Stick 4K. l
